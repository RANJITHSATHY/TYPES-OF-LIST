    Making of Tea

  1. Boil Water: Fill a kettle with fresh, cold water and bring to a boil.
  2. Prepare Mug: Place a tea bag into your mug.
  3. Pour and Steep: Pour the hot water into the mug and let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Remove Tea: Remove the tea bag, squeezing it against the side of the mug for extra flavor.
  5. Customize: Add milk, sugar, lemon, or honey as desired.
